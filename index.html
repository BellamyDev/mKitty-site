how it works

A garden, not a grind.

Five steps. Fifteen minutes a day. Scroll to walk through a session.

↓
step one

Start at your farm.

Every session begins here — a quiet plot of land, a few seeds in your pocket, and trees you've already grown. No login walls. No ads. No streak-shaming popups.

step two

Travel to a biome.

Each MCAT subject is its own world. Biology is a rainforest. Physics is a mountain. CARS is a cave with stories to read. Pick a path and explore.

step three

Learn at checkpoints.

Watch a Khan Academy video, take notes in the in-game notebook, then answer a few questions to prove you've got it. Wrong? No penalty — just try again.

step four

Collect a seed.

Finish the biome and you'll receive a seed for that subject. Bring it home, plant it in your farm, and the real game begins.

step five

Grow it daily.

Water your tree by answering review questions every day. It'll grow from sapling to towering tree as you build mastery — and you can decorate it with ornaments earned along the way.
